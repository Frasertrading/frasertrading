HOW IT WORKS

Everyone could use some extra cash in their pockets, so why not declutter your room or dorm of unused books you no longer have use for, and turn them in to cash! Its widley known that Colleges and Universities try to pump out as many new textbook editions as possible every semester so students have to purchase new editions - because of this, textbooks drop in value rapidly as they quickly become outdated, so its wise to sell your books as quickly as possible to get back the most money.

FOUR EASY STEPS

Selling with Textbookblitz couldnt be easier, below are the four steps taken to sell your books.

1) List the ISBNs of the books you have for sell on our sell textbooks page. You will see a confirmation window pop up, this means we have recieved your form.

2) A human will review your form and reply back to you by email with a quote for your books as well as ask for your shipping details and payment preferences. Most replies are within a few short hours, but can at times take up to 24 hours.

3) If you accept our quote we will reply back to you with a prepaid shipping label which can be used to ship your books for free with Canada Post. All you need to do now is print off the shipping label, put your books in a sturdy box or bubble mailer and tape the label to the outside of your parcel. After this, drop your package off to a local Canada Post location and you're all done. All you have to do is wait for your payment!

4) Once we recieve your books a quality control member will inspect your books and confirm they match our condition guidelines. Afterwards a payment and confirmation email will be sent to you. We offer same-day payments, but please be patient, we sometimes dont get through all packages until the end of the day.

TIPS

  • Please ship your books as soon as possbile, the quicker we recieve your books that quicker you will be paid. We will inspect shipping label activity and if the books are not shipped within 7 days we will send you a reminder email. If we do not hear a response your label will be cancelled.
  • This may sound like common knowledge, but its wise to double check you're mailing the correct books. We have had people accidently mail us the wrong books before and we will not pay for the books that were not supposed to be shipped.
  • Ship your books using a sturdy box or bubble mailer to ensure your books will not be damaged in transit. Do not use oversized boxes without proper insulation as books will move around during transit and they can become damaged. Rolling paper around your book is not sufficent to protect it in transit.
