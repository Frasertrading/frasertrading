Privacy Policy


Last Updated: July 14th, 2016

Textbookblitz.com respects your privacy and will do all that we can in order to ensure that we earn and keep your trust. This Privacy Policy will outline how we collect, use, and protect the information that you provide to us when visiting our site. By using this Site, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

Information we Collect

You may opt to provide personal information to Textbookblitz.com through this site. This information may include your name, e-mail address, shipping address(es), phone number. You may be asked to provide this data when requesting a quote or using our contact form. Users may browse the Site in order to view our products and services without committing to providing any personal information.

How Information is Used

We will use personal information to fulfill your orders for textbook purchases (and related services), provide customer support, and further communicate with you.

Textbookblitz will not under any circumstances disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent. We do not sell customer lists or any other customer information for marketing purposes, and will not do so without your written permission.

We may share personal information we collect strictly for business purposes with companies that provide administrative, shipping and marketing services for us or on our behalf. These individuals or companies are permitted, by contract, only to provide the specific services we have requested of them.

Textbookblitz.com maintains no responsibility for the privacy practices or content of any websites linked through this Site. In the event that you click on a banner or link to an alternate website, you will be subject to the privacy policies of these third party websites.

In certain cases, Textbookblitz may share your personal information as required, or permitted by law. This may include sharing your personal information with third parties if we believe this is necessary to defend our legal rights, defend against legal claims, or otherwise protect the rights and property of Textbookblitz.com or any of our suppliers.

Protecting Your Privacy

Textbookblitz.com employs reasonable security measures and practices to ensure that the personal information submitted to us remains protected, both during transmission and once received. All servers are protected by firewalls, and are maintained on secure premises.

Testimonials

Textbookblitz may post testimonials on our Site that contain personally identifiable information about a user, including their name, school and photo. We will request permission from all user's prior to posting their testimonial within our website. In the event our customer decides they wish to remove their testimonial, we will comply with this request immediately.

Changes

By using Textbookblitz.com, you agree to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy. If this Privacy Policy is amended in the future, we will post all changes here in order to ensure that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we utilize this information, and under which circumstances it will be disclosed. We encourage you to visit this page frequently, as we reserve the right to modify the contents of our Privacy Policy at any time.

Questions?
